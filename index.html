I found my purpose with writing, food and yoga—roughly in that order. The Dharma Kitchen is about cooking and living mindfully and with intent, an idea that is borne equally out of my time in the kitchen and on the mat. These zones are not so different, for they are where we get to try new things, stretch our limits, experiment, and use our creativity. The Dharma Kitchen is a place where I share recipes, stories and ideas, and invite other like-minded individuals to do the same, too. We’re all connected, you know?
